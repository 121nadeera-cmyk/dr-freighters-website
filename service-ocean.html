Comprehensive Sea Freight Solutions

Dr. Freighters offers extensive ocean freight services, connecting you to every major port in the world. Whether you are shipping full container loads (FCL) or less than container loads (LCL), we optimize your routing for the best balance of cost and speed.

Our experienced team handles everything from booking cargo space to managing complex documentation, ensuring your goods clear customs smoothly and arrive at their destination without delay.

Our Ocean Services Include:

  • Full Container Load (FCL)
  • Less than Container Load (LCL) Consolidation
  • Breakbulk and Out-of-Gauge Cargo
  • Port-to-Port and Door-to-Door Delivery
