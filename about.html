Your Trusted Logistics Partner in Sri Lanka

D.R. Freighters is a dynamic company in the international Freight Forwarding business. DRF provides you a descriptive solution for all your clearing and forwarding needs, along with comprehensive cargo transportation services to any destination in Sri Lanka.

We are licensed Customs Brokers and a Class A International Forwarding Company in Sri Lanka.
