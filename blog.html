Seamless Customs Clearance in Sri Lanka: Let Us Handle Your Import Process

Importing goods into Sri Lanka doesn't have to be a complicated or stressful experience. The regulatory landscape and documentation requirements are constantly changing, which is why partnering with a licensed Customs House Agent (CHA) is the smartest move for your business.

As your dedicated customs broker, we take the hassle out of the clearance process. Here is how we manage the journey of your cargo from arrival to final release:

1

Complete Registration & Setup

Before you can import, your business must be registered with Sri Lanka Customs. We guide you through the process of obtaining your TIN and VAT registrations and ensure your company is properly enrolled in the ASYCUDA World system so that trade information flows smoothly.

2

Accurate HS Code Classification & Valuation

Selecting the correct Harmonized System (HS) Code is critical.it determines your duty rates and applicable regulations. Our expert team classifies your goods precisely according to the official Customs Tariff Guide and assists with proper valuation to ensure you never overpay or face compliance penalties.

3

Document Preparation & Declaration (CUSDEC)

Paperwork is where most delays happen, but not on our watch. Once your goods arrive and the Delivery Order is obtained from the shipping agent, we handle the comprehensive preparation and electronic submission of your Customs Declaration (CUSDEC) directly on your behalf.

4

Duty Calculation & Payment Processing

We calculate all applicable duties and taxes upfront so there are no surprises. We guide you through the payment process, ensuring all customs duties and standard administrative requirements are settled promptly and correctly via approved banking channels.

5

Rapid Cargo Clearance & Final Release

Whether your cargo is assigned to the Green, Yellow, or Red channel, we represent you on the ground. We manage any necessary physical customs examinations, ensure full legal compliance, and secure the final release of your goods so they can be delivered to your facility without delay.

Why choose us?

We ensure your goods meet all mandatory laws while minimizing delays, avoiding penalties, and protecting your bottom line.
